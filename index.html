3rd Year Student

In the 7th sem students are eligible for campus placement. TCS, Cognizant, Capgemini, Wipro, Elastic Run, tech Mahendra, Amazon, and so many companies come. The average package is about 4LPA and the highest package is about 20LPA. 70-80% of students are placed in campus placement.

From 6 th semester of your degree, you will eligible to appear for campus interview provided that you have successfully meet the criteria of that company. Many MNC companies visited our college such as TCS, Infosys, wipro and many more. They took an aptitude test if you pass that test then you have to go for interview the process is so simple. In our college 50 to 70 percent students gets placed in Campus Placement. I decided to get Placed in Infosys company while I was in 2nd year of my engineering but I got selected in TCS that time.
